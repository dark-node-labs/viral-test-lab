Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Quick Test Hub may use cookies and similar technologies such as local storage, pixels, tags, and device identifiers.

Quick Test Hub does not require an account, password, or payment card. Cookies are mainly used for analytics, site improvement, security, advertising measurement, and ad personalization where permitted by law.

Types of cookies and similar technologies

Essential technologies may be used by hosting, security, or browser features to deliver pages and protect the site. Analytics technologies help understand aggregate usage, such as pages visited, device type, approximate location, browser type, scroll behavior, and interaction patterns.

Advertising technologies may be used if ads are shown. Advertising partners may use cookies or identifiers to deliver ads, limit frequency, measure performance, detect invalid activity, and personalize ads where legally allowed.

Services currently referenced

Quick Test Hub uses Google Analytics through Google tag infrastructure to measure aggregate site usage. The site also uses Microsoft Clarity to understand page behavior such as clicks, scrolling, and general interaction patterns. If Google AdSense or other ads are displayed, advertising partners may set their own cookies under their policies.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. You can also use browser privacy tools, private browsing modes, and advertising controls provided by Google or other ad platforms. Blocking cookies may not stop every similar technology, and some features or measurements may behave differently.

Relationship to privacy policy

This page should be read together with the Privacy Policy, which explains local browser-tool processing, hosting logs, analytics, advertising, third-party services, and contact options.

Updates

This policy may be updated when the site changes analytics, advertising, hosting, or privacy practices. Continue using the site only if you are comfortable with the current policy.
