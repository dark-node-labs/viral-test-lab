Mouse click test vs CPS test

A mouse click test and CPS test usually measure the same thing: total clicks divided by the test duration. This page uses a quick 5-second format.

How to compare click speed

Use the same mouse, browser, and hand position for fair comparisons. Repeat several runs and compare your average, not only your best burst.
