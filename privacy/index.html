Privacy Policy

Quick Test Hub quizzes and tools run in your browser. The site does not require login, payment, or account creation.

Quiz answers are used locally to calculate your result. Do not submit personal information through quiz answers or browser tool inputs.

Quiz answers and result cards

The SBTI Test, Rice Purity Test, and IQ-style quiz are designed to calculate results on the page. Results may be displayed in your browser so you can read or share them, but you should avoid posting sensitive details publicly.

Accounts and payments

Quick Test Hub does not require an account, password, payment card, or profile. If that changes in the future, this policy should be updated before those features are launched.

Hosting and basic logs

Like most websites, hosting providers may process basic technical logs such as request time, IP address, browser type, and requested URL for security, abuse prevention, debugging, and delivery. These logs are separate from quiz answers.

Analytics

Quick Test Hub uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as pages visited, approximate location, device type, browser type, and interaction events. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to measure visits and help improve the site.

Quick Test Hub also uses Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with pages, such as clicks, scrolling, and general page behavior. Clarity may collect technical information such as device, browser, and session data. Do not enter sensitive personal information into quiz answers or tool fields.

Advertising and cookies

Quick Test Hub may display ads from Google AdSense or other advertising partners. Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, IP address, browser information, page URL, and interaction data to deliver, limit, measure, and personalize ads where permitted by law.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Google provides advertising controls and opt-out options through its ad settings and related privacy tools.

For more detail about cookies and similar technologies, read the Cookie Policy.

Third-party services

Third-party services may process information under their own privacy policies. Current third-party services include Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Google Tag Manager infrastructure, hosting and security providers, and advertising partners if ads are shown.

Contact and updates

For privacy questions, corrections, or policy requests, contact Quick Test Hub at contact@quicktesthub.com or use the Contact page.

Review this page periodically for changes. Use the site only if you are comfortable with this privacy approach.
