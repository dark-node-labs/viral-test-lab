Privacy Policy

Quick Test Hub tools run in your browser. The site does not require login, payment, or account creation.

Do not submit real personal information, production credentials, private customer data, or other secrets through browser tool inputs.

Local tool and device processing

Timing, typing, keyboard, mouse, and gamepad results are calculated in the active page from browser events. Microphone and webcam checks require browser permission and process the live stream in the current session to display a level meter or preview. Quick Test Hub does not intentionally create or upload microphone recordings, webcam snapshots, key logs, or controller histories through these tools.

QA generators create sample output from the options entered on the page. Generated data should remain fictional and should be reviewed before use.

Accounts and payments

Quick Test Hub does not require an account, password, payment card, or profile. If that changes in the future, this policy should be updated before those features are launched.

Hosting and basic logs

Like most websites, hosting providers may process basic technical logs such as request time, IP address, browser type, and requested URL for security, abuse prevention, debugging, and delivery. These logs are separate from the live device streams processed by browser tools.

Analytics

Quick Test Hub uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as pages visited, approximate location, device type, browser type, and interaction events. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to measure visits and help improve the site.

Quick Test Hub also uses Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with pages, such as clicks, scrolling, and general page behavior. Clarity may collect technical information such as device, browser, and session data. Do not enter sensitive personal information into tool fields.

Advertising and cookies

Quick Test Hub may display ads from Google AdSense or other advertising partners. Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, IP address, browser information, page URL, and interaction data to deliver, limit, measure, and personalize ads where permitted by law.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. For Google advertising controls, visit Google Ads Settings. You can also review interest-based advertising choices at AboutAds.info.

For more detail about cookies and similar technologies, read the Cookie Policy.

Third-party services

Third-party services may process information under their own privacy policies. Current third-party services include Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Google Tag Manager infrastructure, hosting and security providers, and advertising partners if ads are shown.

Contact and updates

For privacy questions, corrections, or policy requests, contact Quick Test Hub at contact [at] quicktesthub.com or use the Contact page.

Review this page periodically for changes. Use the site only if you are comfortable with this privacy approach.
