How the double click test works

The tool listens for browser click and double-click events inside the test area. It counts total clicks, double-click events, and the fastest close pair detected during the 10-second window.

How to check for unwanted double-clicking

If you suspect your mouse is double-clicking by itself, use slow single clicks during the test. If double-click events appear even when you are trying to single-click, repeat the test and compare with another mouse or browser.

Double click test vs click speed test

A click speed test measures how many clicks you can make per second. A double click test focuses on close click pairs and browser double-click events, which makes it more useful for mouse troubleshooting.

Double Click Test FAQ

What does a double click test measure?

It records regular click events, browser double-click events, and the fastest interval between close click pairs.

Can this test diagnose a broken mouse?

It can help spot unwanted double-click behavior in the browser, but hardware diagnosis should be confirmed with repeated tests, another browser, and system mouse settings.

Why do intentional fast clicks count as double clicks?

Browsers fire double-click events when two clicks happen close together, whether the cause is intentional clicking or a mouse switch issue.
