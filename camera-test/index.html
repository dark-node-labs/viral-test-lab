Camera test vs webcam test

Camera test and webcam test searches usually describe the same online check: confirm that the browser can access the camera and display a live preview.

Why is the camera blocked?

Camera access can be blocked by browser permission, operating system privacy settings, another app using the camera, or a missing camera device.
