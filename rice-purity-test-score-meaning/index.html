Rice Purity Test Score Meaning

Your Rice Purity score is usually a number from 0 to 100. A higher score means you checked fewer experiences. A lower score means you checked more experiences. It is a casual quiz result, not a judgment of character.

Score Ranges

90-100: Very high score

You may have had fewer listed experiences. This can be common for people who are new to dating, parties, or adult social settings.

70-89: High score

You have probably tried a few common experiences while skipping many others.

40-69: Middle score

This range suggests a mixed set of experiences. Many quiz takers land somewhere around the middle.

0-39: Low score

You checked many items. The result should still be treated as entertainment, not a label or moral ranking.

What Is the Rice Purity Test?

The Rice Purity Test is a viral purity test format that asks yes/no questions about social life, dating, parties, and personal experiences. The score is often shared with friends as a conversation starter.

Is a High or Low Score Better?

Neither. Rice Purity score meaning depends on context, age, culture, comfort level, and personal choices. A score can be funny to compare, but it should not be used to judge someone.
