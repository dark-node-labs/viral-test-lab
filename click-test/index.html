Click test vs CPS test

A click test and a CPS test usually mean the same thing: click as many times as possible during a fixed timer, then divide total clicks by seconds. This page uses a 5-second format so results are quick to repeat.

How to improve click speed

Keep your hand relaxed, use a comfortable mouse, and compare attempts on the same device. For hardware issues like double-clicking or scroll wheel problems, use the full Mouse Test.
