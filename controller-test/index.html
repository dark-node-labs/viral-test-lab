Controller tester vs gamepad tester

Controller tester, controller test, and gamepad tester searches usually mean the same browser tool: connect a controller, press a button, and inspect live button and axis values.

How to check controller drift

Start the tester and leave the sticks untouched. Axis values should sit close to 0.00. Values that stay far from zero may indicate drift or calibration issues.
