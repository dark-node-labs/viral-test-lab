What is the Rice Purity Test?

The Rice Purity Test is a classic checklist-style score quiz. It asks whether you have had different social, romantic, party, online, and life experiences, then turns the answers into a score from 0 to 100. Higher scores usually mean fewer checked experiences, while lower scores mean more checked experiences.

How the score works

Each "yes" answer reduces the score by one point. A 100 means none of the listed experiences were checked, while a 0 means every listed experience was checked. Most people land somewhere between those extremes, and the exact number is less important than the score band and the conversation it starts.

Privacy and safety note

Answer only if you are comfortable. You do not need to prove your score to anyone, and you should avoid sharing details that could identify you or someone else. Some questions can feel personal, so treat the quiz as optional entertainment and stop at any time.

Question topics covered in this purity quiz

The 100-question checklist covers social experiences, dating, relationship moments, parties, online behavior, personal choices, and everyday life situations. The page keeps the test private in your browser and focuses on broad score interpretation instead of asking you to publish sensitive details.

How to interpret your result

Use the score as a range, not as a fixed identity. A 90-100 score, a 70-89 score, a 40-69 score, and a 0-39 score can each reflect age, culture, privacy, opportunity, preference, or mood. The number is most useful when paired with the Rice Purity score meaning guide.
